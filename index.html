I'm a M.S. Artificial Intelligence student at Northeastern's Khoury College (expected 2027), building systems that solve real e-commerce problems — the kind eBay, Amazon, and Shopify face at millions-of-listings scale.

My e-commerce ML portfolio covers entity resolution (matching product listings that describe the same item), hierarchical taxonomy classification (automatically categorizing listings into 3-level paths), named entity recognition (extracting brand, model, color, size from raw titles), and image quality scoring (rating product photos and diagnosing specific issues for sellers).

I also build LLM observability infrastructure (AI Guardian) and multi-agent NLP systems. Previously built and evaluated multi-agent pipelines at Schneider Electric.
